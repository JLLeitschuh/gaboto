{@link org.oucs.gaboto.entities.pool.GabotoEntityPool}s are collections of {@link org.oucs.gaboto.entities.GabotoEntity}s. {@link org.oucs.gaboto.entities.pool.GabotoEntityPool}s can be generated automatically from {@link org.oucs.gaboto.model.GabotoSnapshot}s or Jena Models.

An {@link org.oucs.gaboto.entities.pool.GabotoEntityPool} can be seen as the internal representation of a snapshot of RDF. {@link org.oucs.gaboto.entities.pool.GabotoEntityPool}s can be transformed into RDF or other output formats such as KML.

For most queries to the Gaboto system an {@link org.oucs.gaboto.entities.pool.GabotoEntityPool} is created that is then transformed into the demanded output format.
